School Crush

When I looked around and saw the mess, I realised moving to a new city is not my cup of tea. I've been trying to arrange the house for last two hours, yet nothing is on it's place. So I decided to take a break instead. I took my phone and started looking for matches in this new city on 'Match Maker'. Strange! I found 'Kartik' there. My classmate from school and my childhood crush. But he was with Shalini I guess. I swiped right to know what's actually happening in his life. I got a match. It was quick so I sent a 'Hi'. "'Kritika? Is it you?," was his reply. Damn! He remembers me. And the conversation continued. We talked about our friends, teachers and school memories. Precious! Aren't they? I asked him about Shalini and got to know that they broke up about a year ago because of some trust issues. Yeah, I got excited and asked him for a date. Afterall he was my crush. I know I'm an idiot. He didn't reply. For a while I thought I ruined everything but then my phone buzzed. There was a message from him,"Sorry! Got a phone call from my office and 'Classic Café' is okay for you?" Next day I dressed to impress. I reach the café, where he was already waiting for me. It was great feeling meeting him after so long. We talked for an hour. The time passed like wind. I had to clear my doubts so I couldn't stop myself from asking,"So, after Shalini, did you date someone?" "A few, but none of them were as good as my crush," he said.
"Shalini, right?," I said.
"She was never my crush. We were friends at first and later dated eachother. I had crush on someone else from the class. Infact I used to copy her answers during exams," he said.
"But you used to copy from my answer sheet," I said.
It hit me late. He had crush on me. Imagine, your crush has crush on you. I was on the cloud nine. I blushed.
